About Me

I am an assistant professor of political science at the College of Charleston. I am also affiliated with the Sustainability Literacy Institute and I am a research fellow with the Center for Public Choice and Market Process.

My research and teaching interest are in public policy, with a specific interest in environmental and energy policy. Much of my work is focused on climate change and nuclear energy.
